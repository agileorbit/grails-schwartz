Package: com.agileorbit.schwartz.util

[Groovy] Class SchedulerSnapshot

    • com.agileorbit.schwartz.util.SchedulerSnapshot

    • Captures data from several areas of Quartz for use with testing or debugging. Most of the source objects (the Scheduler, the JobStore, etc.) aren't retained - a relevant subset of the available information is stored here. Most everything is copied/cloned and/or immutable, but modifying anything should avoided and the data should be considered read-only.
